## Welcome to the TPM Learning Series

This site is aimed at helping Technical Program Managers (TPMs) onboard into new and emerging technologies, including:

- **Machine Learning & AI** — core concepts, terminology, and how ML systems are built and operated
- **Large Language Models (LLMs)** — how they work, where they excel, and their limitations
- **AI-Assisted Development** — new patterns emerging from AI tooling in the software development lifecycle, such as Spectre event development

Whether you are new to these areas or looking to deepen your understanding, this series provides approachable, practitioner-focused content to help you lead and contribute to AI-driven projects with confidence.
